Behind the technology that makes Source possible.

Utilizing the power of blockchain

Source is a blockchain powered network for internet access. By incentivizing every internet connected individual to share their internet access, we’re not only increasing the connectivity of digital consumers worldwide but also minimizing the costs of wireless access and communication. We are currently building on top of the Bitcoin and Ethereum blockchain, to power payments, authentication, and our registry of verified internet providers. We're building a platform to bring internet infrastructure into the modern age.

Our Network

For Providers

Source differentiates itself from current internet service providers by rewarding any individual who publicizes their wireless network and/or mobile device‘s access. Payments are directly transferred from a network guest to a Source Provider. Payments will be closely tied to the Source Network Token. We’re establishing a single standardized token capable of purchasing internet access from any Source Provider. Our network is not owned by the community uncontrolled by any single entity and fully managed by the individuals contributing to the strength and reach of the network.

For Guests

By downloading our Source App on all your devices, you never have to worry about being without internet access anywhere you are in the world. Never worry about needing to buy a local SIM card, or messing with network settings, you’re automatically connected with Source.

Infrastructure and Hardware

For Providers

With Source, you own your hardware. If you want to download and install your own router, you’re free to do so. Or purchase one of our pre-configured routers! We welcome hardware enthusiasts to build their own hardware and enable the Source network. Since we have no centralized infrastructure, payments will always be able to be processed.

For Guests

You’re not locked into only using our network. When you’re not in range of a Source Provider, we automatically transfer you to a different cellular or WiFi if available.

Fair Pricing

For Providers

Wireless access deserves a pay-as-you-go model of transacting. Current pricing schemes are outdated, inefficient, and favor centralization of large monopolistic Internet service providers. We‘ve chosen a blockchain architecture to take advantage of payment channel technology to facilitate fast and scalable micropayments. Essentially, it makes more sense to establish a contract with the internet provider you want internet access from only when you need internet access. You only pay for the time that you use.

For Guests

With this model, everyday consumers save money on their net internet expenses. Combined with the proliferation of Source Network Providers, internet access becomes more available for everyone in the world. When you travel to a new Provider, you’re instantly able to use the remaining time on in your account.

Source Network Token

For Guests and Providers

Wireless access is a commodity. That's why we created the Source Network Token. As our network grows in value, the token grows in value. You’ll be able to purchase more internet with the same amount of token. How cool is that!

Easily cash in and out of Source Network Token

We integrate directly with the most trusted payments providers. This allows you to allow you to fund and withdraw from within the Source app. We integrate with Coinbase and any other wallet provider, as well as traditional payments gateways with Paypal, Stripe, AliPay, and MPesa. It might seem a bit complicated, but we’ve worked out a solution so all you have to pay for is data.

User and Transaction Data

For Providers

While user data is totally safe and secure, you can still run analytics on anonymized session data that have come from your local router. With this data, you can change the physical location of your router to increase transactions. For others interested in this data, all they have to do is use Source Network Token to get access. All session initiations, session lengths, and session locations are stored. We think that this data will help providers, researchers, and companies alike learn more about internet usage habits worldwide. We hope analysis of the data lets companies place Source Provider Networks in locations where there is high demand.

For Guests

Traditional internet providers keep a lot of your payment, user information, and sometimes browsing data. With Source, all your data stays on your local devices. There’s no way that your personal information can get out. We treat our user data very seriously. While the information used to get access to a Source router is stored locally on that router, the rest of your information is stored on your own device. This means your personal information like age, wallet information, browsing habits can’t be accessed by anyone but you.
